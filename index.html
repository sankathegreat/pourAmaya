Words I Almost Never Said

✦
🪶

Amaya, I have started this letter so many times.

Crumpled pages. Wrong words. Long pauses where I just sat there,
pen in hand, wondering if you would ever understand
how hard it is to say this out loud — or even on paper.

I am not good at this. I want you to know that first.
I am nervous even now, writing to someone who has no idea
what they have quietly done to me.

You probably do not notice the small things.
How I look for you in a room before I have even decided to.
How I laugh a little louder when you are nearby, hoping you will turn around.
How I replay conversations with you — not because something went wrong,
but because I just want to hear them again.

I do not know if this is the right time.
I am not even sure I am brave enough to give this to you.
But I wanted — just once — to say it honestly.

✦   But listen carefully, okay?   ✦

You matter to me, Amaya.
More than I know how to say without sounding like a complete fool…
which, unfortunately, I already am.

And the worst part?
I would still choose you even if I had a chance
to undo all of this overthinking.

So go ahead. Laugh. Tease me. Call me an idiot.
Just don't act like you didn't feel something too, you idiot.

And I am terrified that you will read this and smile politely.
But I am more terrified of never having said it at all.

And everything I could not say in words… is sealed below. 💜
